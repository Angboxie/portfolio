Tactibrawl is a pixel-art tactical brawler designed in Figma. Two players place heroes on a grid and battle for arena control.

I led the UI across a 5-person team. Splash, loading, hero select, tutorial, and battle arena. Characters were hand-drawn in pixel art. Environments were AI-generated and art-directed.

The full game is prototyped and interactive in Figma.

// PROCESS

HOW WE
BUILT IT.

  • 01
    GAME CONCEPT + UX FLOW
    Mapped the full loop as a team. Splash, hero select, tutorial, grid placement, battle. Every screen defined before design started.
  • 02
    ART DIRECTION
    Pixel characters on AI-generated painterly environments. Gold as the primary UI accent. Fantasy-native without going generic RPG.
  • 03
    CHARACTER DESIGN
    Characters drawn pixel by pixel. Tank, Healer, Mage, and Sola. Strong silhouettes readable at small grid sizes during battle.
  • 04
    ENVIRONMENTS
    AI-generated backgrounds, heavily curated. Forest arena, waterfall canyon, training room. Each refined to sit alongside pixel art without clash.
  • 05
    TUTORIAL SYSTEM
    Dialogue-driven tutorial with Sola as the guide. Multiple expressions react to context. Parchment dialogue boxes match the game world.
  • 06
    FULL PROTOTYPE
    Every screen wired with interactions and transitions. Fully playable from title to battle in Figma.
