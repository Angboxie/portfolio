The phone case market is flooded with corporate mass-production. We saw something different, where local Australian artists making incredible work with no platform built for them at scale. So we built one.

Casepop AU puts independent creators front and centre. Every design ships from an Australian artist. We handle the store and growth. They get the spotlight.

I co-founded the brand and owned all front-end work: custom coding on top of Shopify to build exactly what the brand needed, not what a template offered.

Process

HOW WE
DID IT.

  • 01
    Brand Identity from Scratch
    Name, logo, colour system, tone of voice, all built from zero. Needed to feel youthful and community-led without overshadowing the artist work it was built to promote.
  • 02
    Shopify + Custom Front End
    Shopify as the commerce engine, then custom Liquid, HTML, CSS and JS on top. Overrode the template everywhere it got in the way: product pages, collection layouts, artist feature sections, the nav.
  • 03
    Artist Onboarding
    Built a simple submission and onboarding process for local creators. Multiple artists onboarded, each with their own collection and full attribution on site.
  • 04
    UGC + Organic Social
    Grew to 1.5K+ through UGC: real customers, real photos, real community. Zero paid ads. Built artist and customer relationships that fed back into product drops.
  • 05
    Ongoing Iteration
    Monitored behaviour and iterated continuously: tightening product pages, streamlining checkout, making artist stories more prominent across the purchase flow.
